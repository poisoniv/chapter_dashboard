# chapter_dashboard

- portal for chapters to login and manage their membership roster, finances (dues), submit documents/paperwork

member from a chapter can login and view/edit their chapter's roster, see status of payments (and amounts), and submit forms/paperwork for review by national officers

- admin portal with access to all chapters

national officers can login and see all chapter rosters, their associated payment status, and review any documents they've uploaded

- integration with g-suite (sign-ins, drive, calendar, etc)

national officers should be able to login with their g-suite credentials. files should be able to be linked from google drive.
